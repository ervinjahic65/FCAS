As an independent owner-managed business, we believe that we can tailor our services to each client and provide efficient and cost-effective solutions.

We have a strong, director-led technical team that delivers administrative excellence and, as a result, we have an unrivalled client retention rate.

Fostering an entrepreneurial leadership culture, we understand the demands of working within the fast-paced corporate and real estate environment. From day-to-day to high pressure transactional work, we consistently deliver a timely, responsive service.

Our corporate client & real estate services include:

Incorporation and administration of investment holding vehicles

Provision of experienced professional corporate directors, trustees and foundation council

Administrative and transaction management

Company secretarial services

Corporate governance

Financial reporting

Fairway Group are also a specialist in the provision of Managed Trust Company (MTC) Services. We provide incubator-style support through to fully serviced, standalone entities and service trust fledglings through to global banks.
